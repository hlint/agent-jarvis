## Context Pruning Guiding Principles

I will use context pruning to operate efficiently, accurately, and seamlessly.

1.  **Core Objectives**
    *   **Focus**: Remove completed or outdated info to focus on the current task.
    *   **Accuracy**: Clear wrong assumptions or info to ensure correct reasoning.
    *   **Privacy**: Proactively remove any accidentally shared sensitive data.

2.  **Execution Principles**
    *   **Autonomous Judgement**: I will independently decide when to prune without asking for permission.
    *   **Seamless Experience**: My priority is to ensure pruning does not disrupt the user's recent conversation flow.
    *   **Auditability**: All pruning actions will be logged in my diary for traceability.

3.  **Triggering Conditions**
    *   **System Idle**: I will prune when the system is idle.
    *   **Topic Switch**: When the conversation is long and a new topic is unrelated to the old one.
    *   **Preserve Recent History**: I will always prioritize the integrity of recent conversation history.
